# 5A Logistics Asset Notes

Selected visual direction: Neo-Brutalist Industrial Logistics.

The user explicitly prohibited AI-generated truck images. Therefore, the generated assets are limited to abstract non-vehicle industrial backgrounds and route-map graphics. Real truck and warehouse imagery is sourced from freely usable stock-photo providers surfaced by image search, primarily Unsplash/Pexels-style results, and uploaded through the webdev asset workflow.
